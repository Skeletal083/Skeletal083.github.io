A Basic Introduction To My Ocs!

Skeletal

Skeletal is a humanoid being, her species is unknown, but a defining feature is her pointed ears and naturally bright orange hair. She has pointed and sharp teeth and a scar along her left shoulder. She is close friends with Squirt and Nymph.She also had a brother named Ghostly who is no longer alive. She is a somewhat supernatural being and due to this her appearence can change to include:

  • Retractable Claws
  • Lengthened Tongue
  • Longer Ears
  • Sharper Teeth

Squirt

Squirt is Skeletal's best friend. She is a human female that got sacrificed to the ink and her story is derived from the Bendy and The Ink Machine games. She has ink for hair and cartoony eyes. She has deformed horns and a melting halo. Her arms are also often stained with ink spots from traversing the messy studio for so long. She met Skeletal when she was saved by her from the Ink Demon's attacks. Ever since, the two have been inseperable. She met Nymph a few years later, but they are also quite close.

Nymph

Nymph doesn't really have a backstory to tell so I'm just going to describe her. Nymph is a fairy like being with blue and gold butterfly wings. She has short black hair with a natural gold streak in the front. She has gold flakes on her skin and a moon shaped birthmark. She has the abilitly to shoot gold strings out of each of her fingertips that are incredibly durable. She is friends with Squirt and Skeletal, but her and Skeletal tease eachother a lot.

Ghostly

Ghostly was Skeletal's little brother who passed away at the age of 8 for unknown reasons. He has shaggy blue hair and freckles. He was the same species as skeletal, but it is still unknown. He also had a tail and claws, though he didn't know how to control his abilities like Skeletal, so he didn't know how to retract them.
